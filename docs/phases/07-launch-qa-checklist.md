# 07 Launch QA Checklist

Before production launch:
- Run lint, typecheck, content check, and build.
- Verify homepage, shop, product detail, cart, checkout, and COA pages.
- Confirm top banner, product card, and checkout attestation placements.
- Replace mock products and sample COA records with approved supplier data.
- Confirm WooCommerce provider mode in staging before production.
- Confirm hosted checkout redirect in staging before production.
- Confirm ShipStation workflow in staging before production.
